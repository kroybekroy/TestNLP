Transaction
-----------
Organization : Address of SMS and Organization name in SMS
Date and Time of Transaction : Date and Time of Reception of SMS and Date and Time of Transaction in SMS
Place of Transaction : Place of Reception of SMS and Place of Transaction in SMS
Type of Place of Transaction : ATM, Restaurant, Shopping Mall e.t.c
Type of Transaction : Debit, Credit
Account Type : Credit Card, Debit Card, Savings Account e.t.c : Preceeds Account Number 
Account Number : In Integer usually preceeded by X...
Amount : In Decimal Preceeded by Currency
